About Us

Who We Are?

Bellabit is an emerging team specialized in cybersecurity and Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. The team was founded with the goal of developing security skills and promoting cybersecurity culture among those interested in this field.

We are a group of professionals and cybersecurity enthusiasts, working together to face security challenges and test the limits of our knowledge and capabilities. We participate in local and international CTF competitions to hone our skills and exchange knowledge with the global security community.

Our vision is to build a new generation of cybersecurity experts capable of facing evolving cyber threats and protecting the digital space.
